I am passing along some important clarifications from my most recent meeting with LandSeed. These updates may affect some of the work already completed or currently planned, so they should be taken into account when structuring the next tickets.

Key product changes / clarifications from LandSeed

Image generation for modifications:
The platform needs to support image generation so it can generate a visual of what the final modified space would look like. In other words, based on the senior’s requested modification and submitted photos, the system should generate an image showing how the room or section would look once the accessibility modification is incorporated.

AI-generated estimates without a predefined pricing matrix:
The platform needs to provide AI-generated estimates directly to the client.  LandSeed will not be providing the predefined pricing matrix we originally expected, the AI would need to be able to access external pricing information, such as product and merchandise costs from stores like Home Depot, and use that to generate estimates.

AI-driven grant discovery (no predefined rule set):
LandSeed may not provide structured grant-rule data. Instead, their expectation is that the AI should search for and identify the appropriate municipal, provincial, and national grants based on the senior’s situation, project details, and submitted information. In other words, grant matching is expected to be handled largely through AI rather than a fixed rule set provided upfront.
